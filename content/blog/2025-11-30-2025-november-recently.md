## üí™ Health

Started off strong this month with regular running, but then I suddenly started
getting massive headaches after my runs and after sleeping. I've been going to a
physiotherapist the last month to figure out what's giving me the headaches, and
it seems like we connected it to me being "stiff" in the neck and not breathing
correctly because of my posture. Which also affected my sleeping position. I've
had the headaches on-and-off for the last five years, but they have never been
as severe as they've been now. And after figuring this out the headaches have
been absent! Hopefully knowing what seems to be the cause will limit how often
it affects me.

Besides headaches, I also managed to get pretty sick in the end of the month.
Winter is coming.
