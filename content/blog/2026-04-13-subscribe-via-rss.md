Last year I wrote [Subscribe via e-mail](/blog/subscribe-via-email). I made it
possible for people to subscribe to the blog with an e-mail instead of having to
rely on RSS. But now, 13 months later, and $126 poorer, I'm canceling my
Buttondown subscription.

The main reason is money, it's expensive with $9 for just 7 subscribers. I see
in my server logs that a lot more are subscribing with RSS, so I guess that's my
target audience. I even looked into some self-hosted solutions, like Listmonk,
but it didn't have RSS-to-email out of the box and I didn't want to maintain a
list of emails.

The subscription lasts until May 4th, so everything I publish will still be
available as e-mails. But I highly recommend getting an RSS-reader if you want
to continue subscribing to my blog. I use [NetNewsWire](https://netnewswire.com)
daily on both macOS and iOS — it's free and open source. Check out
[How I RSS](/blog/how-i-rss) to find out more about RSS and what other services
and software is out there.
